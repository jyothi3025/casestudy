About This Calorie Calculator

This simple app estimates your BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) using the Mifflinâ€“St Jeor equation and multiplies it by an activity factor to estimate your TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure).

How it works

  • Enter age, weight (kg), height (cm), gender and activity level.
  • The app returns BMR and estimated daily calories to maintain your weight (TDEE).

This project is designed for a DevOps case-study: containerized with Docker, deployed via Kubernetes, and automated with Jenkins CI/CD.
